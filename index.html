What's included

  • Competent Person Service

    Support aligned with Regulation 7 of the Management of Health and Safety at Work Regulations 1999, providing your business with access to a named, competent health & safety advisor. We act as your external safety resource, offering practical guidance to help you meet your legal duties, manage risk, and maintain compliance across your operations.

  • Telephone & Email Advice

    Ongoing access to professional health & safety advice via telephone and email, providing clear, practical guidance when issues arise. Whether you need reassurance, second opinions, or direction on specific situations, support is available on a fair-use basis to help you make informed decisions quickly.

  • Annual H&S Policy Review

    Annual review of your health & safety policy to ensure it remains up to date, relevant to your activities, and aligned with current legislation and best practice. Recommendations will be provided where improvements or updates are required.

  • Basic Risk Assessment Review

    Review and feedback on your existing risk assessments to ensure they are suitable, sufficient, and reflective of your actual working activities. Practical recommendations provided to improve clarity, control measures, and compliance where required. (Creation of new risk assessments available as an additional service.)

  • Legal & Regulatory Updates

    Ongoing updates on relevant changes to health & safety legislation and industry guidance that may impact your business. Key changes are communicated in a clear and practical way, helping you understand what actions (if any) are required..
